## Hardware description

### Joystick

* GND to GND 
* +5V to +5V
* Vrx and Vry we connect to analog pins A0, A1 

### Rotation encoder

* GND to GND
* +5V to +5V
* CLK and DT to digital pins 11, 12
* SW to digital pin 2

### Digilent PmodCLP LCD modul 

* GND to GND 
* VCC to +5V J2
* pins 7,10 to digital pins 4, 7
* RW to GND
* Rs and En to digital pins 8 and 9
